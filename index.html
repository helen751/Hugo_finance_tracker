About Hugo Finance Tracker

Hugo is a web app created by Helen to help ALU students manage their finances effectively.

Hugo is a simple, intuitive finance tracking app designed specifically for ALU students. It helps you manage your finances effectively by allowing you to log expenses, track income, set budgets, and generate reports.

With Hugo, you can easily categorize your spending, monitor your income sources, and stay within your budget limits. The app provides a user-friendly interface and powerful features to help you take control of your financial life.

FEATURES OF HUGO

Hugo offers some exclusive features that helps you manage your finances effectively!

  • Expense Tracking

    Log daily spending, categorize it, and see where your money goes.

  • Income Reporting

    Record stipends, salaries, and transfers; view monthly and total income.

  • Report Generation

    Export clean summaries (JSON/CSV/PDF) for sharing or record-keeping.

  • Budgets & Live Alerts

    Set monthly caps per category and get gentle warnings before you overspend.
